Login as Donor — Key Features

  • Donate Unused Medicines Easily
    Upload details of medicines with expiry date and image.
  • Expiry Date Auto-Validation
    System checks if the medicine is valid before accepting.
  • Track Donation Status
    Know whether your medicine is approved, picked up, or requested by an NGO.
  • Edit or Delete Donations
    Modify details of medicines before NGO approval or remove entries.
  • Notifications
    Receive alerts for pickups, approvals, expiry warnings, or NGO messages.
  • Profile Management
    Update personal info, address, mobile number, and password.
